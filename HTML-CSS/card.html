Apple from Jumla

An apple is a round, edible fruit produced by the apple tree (Malus domestica). Apples come in a variety of colors, primarily red, green, and yellow, and have a crisp, juicy flesh. They are typically sweet or tart in flavor. The fruit's skin is smooth, and it contains seeds at its core. Apples are rich in dietary fiber, vitamin C, and various antioxidants, making them a popular and healthy snack. They can be eaten raw or used in cooking and baking, such as in pies, sauces, and salads. The apple tree is cultivated worldwide and is one of the most widely grown tree fruits.
